ABOUT US

Hopelands cattery is a small family run business offering
boarding for cats and small animals.

Hopelands cattery is a small family run business offering boarding for cats and small animals. Based on a working farm in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire, we have been successfully running since 1996 and offer unrivalled levels of quality care for your cherished pets.

Our aim here at Hopelands is to provide your cat with a home away from home, with comfort and feeding routines the same to which it is at home ensuring a stress free and enjoyable stay for your cat.

The cattery consists of both inside and outdoor chalets. Indoor chalets have individual heaters to each enclosure, whilst outdoor chalets have individual thermostat heaters with a large run for your cat to stretch their legs.

The cattery is run by a family team. Heather, who has an NVQ Level 3 in animal welfare and care, and her husband and son who are on hand to provide extra care where needed ensuring that your cat is left in the best possible hands.
